# Cleft Sentenses

**Cleft sentenses** are often used in spoken and written to add emphasis to a particular element of a sentence

Examples:

- It is the feeling of belonging that makes a home
- It is the people who make a home
- It is not just a feeling that makes a home

Cleft sentense type 1: with verb to be

```jsx
it + verb to be + emphasis/new + that + old/known
```

- It was my home that I missed the most when I immigrate to another country.

Cleft sentense type 2: with WH words

```jsx
what* + known/old information + verb + new information/emphasis
```

- What make me want to immigrate was the prospect of finding a new home and a better life

Exercises

1. **What** makes a home is not just the physical structure, but also the feeling of belonging.
2. **It** was **the memories** we make together that turned our house into a home.
3. **It** is not always about having a perfect home, but rather a comfortable and welcoming one.
4. **How** you decorate your space can greatly impact how you feel about your home.
5. **It** is not just the four walls and a roof that make a house a home.
